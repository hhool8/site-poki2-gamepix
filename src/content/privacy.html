Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This policy explains how GamePix by Poki2 handles analytics, advertising, and limited technical data when you browse or play games on gamepix.poki2.online.

1. Information We Collect

GamePix by Poki2 does not require you to create an account. We collect limited anonymous data through standard web analytics:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Referring URL and approximate geographic region

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us voluntarily.

2. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and to display relevant advertising (Google AdSense and GamePix ads). You can disable cookies in your browser settings; this will not prevent you from playing games.

3. Third-Party Services

GamePix by Poki2 uses the following third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics — traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense — advertising
  • GamePix — game distribution and in-game advertising

Game content is hosted on play.gamepix.com and loaded inside an iframe. GamePix's privacy policy applies to game sessions.

4. Advertising

We display ads from Google AdSense and GamePix's ad network. These services may use cookies and web beacons to show personalised advertisements based on your interests. You can opt out of personalised ads by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

5. Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Questions about this policy? Contact us or email contact@poki2.online.
